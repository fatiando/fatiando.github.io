Contacting us

Our project is developed in the open with the help of our community. Your input is welcome and appreciated. Get in touch!

We strive to make our community welcoming to all. Everyone who participates is expected to follow our Code of Conduct and remember to be excellent to each other 👨🏾‍🎤!

Want to get involved? Take a look at our Contributing Guide to see how you can get involved. This and other guides (authorship policy, project maintenance, etc.) can be found in the fatiando/community repository.

Forum

Participate in the Fatiando community forum to ask and answer questions, get project updates, share your expertise, and showcase your work.

Bugs and features

To report bugs and request features, open an Issue on GitHub. Leave a comment on any issue or pull request to join the conversation.

Chat

Hop on to our ~fatiando channel in Software Underground’s Mattermost instance to talk to users and developers and get to know the community.

Video calls

We meet up every two weeks to discuss project development and share updates. Everyone is welcome to participate! Dates and connection details are in the fatiando/community repository.

Calendar: You also add our shared calendar to get notifications of meetings and other events.
